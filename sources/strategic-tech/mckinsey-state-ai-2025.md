McKinsey's 2025 State of AI survey describes a market where AI usage is widespread but scaled enterprise impact is still emerging. The report says 23 percent of respondents report scaling an agentic AI system somewhere in the enterprise, while another 39 percent have begun experimenting with agents.

The same report notes that enterprise-level EBIT impact remains limited, with only 39 percent reporting EBIT impact at the enterprise level. Agent usage is reported most often in IT and knowledge management functions.

Implication for Crux: enterprise agent demand is plausible, but the evidence supports a staged validation strategy rather than immediate confidence in broad platform adoption.
