Microsoft reports that most leaders plan to integrate agents into AI strategy, while a much smaller share have deployed them organization-wide.

The blog also describes Microsoft 365 Agent Store and Copilot integration, showing that enterprise incumbents are building agent distribution into existing productivity platforms.

Implication for Crux: the startup risk is not only product capability. Incumbent distribution and buyer trust can compress the opportunity for a broad horizontal enterprise agent platform.
