B2B SaaS customers value AI copilot features when they save time, but workflow automation can be more durable when it removes recurring operational bottlenecks.

Customer discovery should segment jobs to be done, admin controls, trust requirements, and measurable time savings before choosing the product bet.

The strongest product strategy signal is willingness to pay, retention impact, or expansion impact from one path rather than press interest.
