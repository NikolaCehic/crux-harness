Limited engineering capacity means building both an AI copilot and workflow automation can dilute quality and delay customer impact.

Workflow automation tied to existing customer jobs may be harder to copy than a generic AI copilot, but it requires deeper integration with current product behavior.

The next two quarters should prioritize the path that wins prototype usage, ROI, and retention tests.
