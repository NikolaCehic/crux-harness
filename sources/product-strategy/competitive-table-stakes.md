Competitors adding generic AI copilots may reset buyer expectations, but table-stakes pressure is not the same as durable differentiation.

User trust, workflow fit, admin controls, and measurable time savings are adoption blockers for both copilot and workflow automation paths.

The product team should avoid building an AI copilot just for positioning unless customer evidence shows stronger willingness to pay or retention impact.
