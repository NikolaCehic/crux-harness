Developer participation depends on predictable approvals, subsidy design, financing availability, construction timing, and building-code constraints.

Permitting delays, neighborhood politics, and construction uncertainty can push housing delivery beyond the city's policy horizon.

A pilot subsidy should target buildings where developer interviews show a clear subsidy threshold and a credible path to completed housing units.
