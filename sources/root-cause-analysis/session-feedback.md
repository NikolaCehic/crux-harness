Session recordings and support tickets can reveal whether users encountered new friction, confusion, or missing context during redesigned onboarding.

The strongest causal evidence would combine a step-level funnel decline with qualitative friction at the same moment in the experience.

The team should diagnose the activation decline with segmented funnel, instrumentation, and session evidence before choosing rollback or iteration.
