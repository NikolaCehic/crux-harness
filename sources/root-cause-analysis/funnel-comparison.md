Segmented funnel comparison can show whether the activation drop after onboarding redesign is concentrated in a specific step, cohort, or traffic source.

If the largest drop appears only after a changed onboarding step, the redesign becomes a stronger causal suspect.

If the decline aligns with acquisition mix, instrumentation changes, or seasonality, the team should avoid rolling back blindly.
