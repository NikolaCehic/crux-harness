US incumbents, brokers, and embedded lending platforms may capture attractive SMB borrowers before a European fintech can build efficient distribution.

Trust, borrower acquisition cost, capital availability, broker incentives, and state-by-state compliance are adoption blockers for a new entrant.

The fintech should test a narrow state, partner, and borrower segment before committing to broad US SMB lending market entry.
