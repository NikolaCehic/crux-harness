Hybrid RAG and long-context systems can combine broad context with targeted retrieval, grounding, access control, and cost control.

Freshness, provenance, permissions, evaluation, and latency are likely adoption blockers for replacing retrieval-augmented generation entirely.

The decision should depend on a benchmark that compares RAG, long-context, and hybrid systems on the enterprise workloads that matter.
