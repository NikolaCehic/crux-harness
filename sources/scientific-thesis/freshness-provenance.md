Retrieval-augmented generation remains useful when enterprise users require freshness, citations, provenance, and modular knowledge updates.

Long context windows reduce some retrieval needs, but they do not automatically solve permissions, source attribution, content freshness, or governance.

The applied AI research team should benchmark RAG, long-context, and hybrid systems before changing infrastructure.
