Limited audited revenue and uncertain pilot-to-paid conversion make the investment case depend on deeper diligence rather than narrative claims about AI compliance automation.

The strongest positive signal would be repeatable expansion from pilots into paid compliance workflows with budget owner sponsorship and low service burden.

The strongest negative signal would be revenue that depends on bespoke implementation, weak retention, or automation accuracy that does not satisfy audit and liability requirements.
